// Selected work — five projects in depth

Projects

01

Sensor technology evaluation for automotive level & quality measurement

Master’s thesis · Robert Bosch GmbH · 2024
▾
Goal
Determine which sensor technologies best measure liquid level and quality in an automotive tank under realistic operating conditions, and recommend one for development.
Test rig
Designed and built a custom laboratory rig around a fluid tank (~143 × 93 × 300 mm, up to 4 L), instrumented for repeatable, controlled measurement.
Test matrix
  • Tilt angles: 0°, 5°, 10° to simulate vehicle attitude
  • Temperature: room temperature and 60 °C for thermal performance
  • Fluid composition: pure water through to a 50% ethanol mix
  • Ultrasonic sensing benchmarked against a calibrated reference sensor
  • Targeted radar and LiDAR scoping experiments to confirm the assessment
Method
Applied a Pugh-matrix approach for structured technology comparison and selection; processed and evaluated all measurement data in Python.
Outcome
Delivered a data-driven technology recommendation to engineering and management, backed by a full characterisation dataset across every test condition.
